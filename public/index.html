Truth WIthin STatistics - Hackathon

As part of the 2018 Swiss Statistics Meeting, a hackathon will be held for the first time. During the hackathon, interested participants will research the potential of various data sets.

When?
August 25 - August 26, 2018
Where?
University of Zurich

Fruitful hackathons depend on fertile ground. Our organizing team is involved in several other events taking place this year, such as MakeZurich vol. 2 and the Wiki-Datathon. TWIST will draw upon the work done at these events.
